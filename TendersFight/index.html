Tenders Fight


Tenders Fight is the first game published on Steam that we have monetized.

It's a fast FPS Game in which you take on the role of a space boxer who must fight against the evil forces of chickens in order to return to earth.

You can use your fists, feet and special abilities to defeat your enemies and escape the space ship.

Try here


What I learnt


  • This was the first time we'd started a project knowing that we were going to publish it for a fee. So we had to produce a clean, professional product.
  • It took us a while to understand the importance of communication, whether in terms of visuals, descriptions or posts on the networks. We did very little of it, even though it's very important to get a game off the ground.
  • We've had positive and negative reviews all over the world and on a whole host of platforms such as YouTube and Twitch, which has also helped us get our game out there!
