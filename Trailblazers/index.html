Trailblazers


Trailblazers is a role-playing game based on the theme of the "World After".

Players take on the role of Trailblazers, mercenaries who have to explore a hostile planet called Uthea, where they have to survive and leave once they have achieved their objectives.

See Rules (PDF)

See Characters (PDF)


What I learnt


  • This project was in paper format, so we didn't have any technical constraints linked to video games. However, it's difficult to know which direction to take. Working as a group was a real challenge for everyone
  • We discovered the role of narrative designer, which is both very free and very complicated. I really enjoyed this first experience of narrative design.
  • We weren't in direct contact with the artists, then we had to create a document of intent so that they could use it to illustrate our game.
